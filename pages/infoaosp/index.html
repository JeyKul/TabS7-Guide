Notes about AOSP and their Recovery

In AOSP its common to use their own shipped Recovery. It's highly limited, because you should use a Recovery for anything that isnt related to userdata. What the AOSP people don't do- provide a .tar for Odin.

We can make it ourselves. You need a Tool, that can pack a .tar. Windows 11 can do it without programs.

Have a file that is named "recovery.img", rightclick, compress to -> .tar. In 7-zip you click add to archive.
